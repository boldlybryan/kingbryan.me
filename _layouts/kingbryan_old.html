You need someone you can trust

Making a web site is easy, but creating an amazing user experience takes advanced knowledge. What is user experience? Simply put, UX is the perception a visitor has after interacting with your products, services, marketing material, social media, and more. This includes the tasteful design of your branding, optimal performance of your site, and having meaningful interaction with the user.

About me

Making websites is my passion. Whether I'm getting paid to complete a project or just tinkering in my spare time, I push myself to become a better webby every day. I take pride in the fact that I can start a wireframe on a blank sheet of paper, write the code to make it all work, and then push it all to the server for the world to see. I come from a business background, so I'm capable of putting together a comprehensive digital strategy to go along with a beautiful design.


When I'm not creating kick-ass stuff for the web, I can be found geeking out on other tech. I'm generally curious about the everyday problems I have that can be solved with technology. Other than that, I try to spend as much time as I can enjoying the great outdoors. Living in Michigan, I have access to just about any recreational activty there is, and some of the best beer in the WORLD. Also, I'm getting married to @ohh_k8, she's pretty rad!
